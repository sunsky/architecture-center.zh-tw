<span data-ttu-id="7b885-109">**迷思：雲端一定比較便宜。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-109">**Myth: Cloud is always cheaper.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-110">許多人都認為在雲端操作資料中心的成本一定比內部部署低廉。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-110">It's a common belief that operating a datacenter in the cloud is always cheaper than on-premises.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-111">這有可能是對的，但並非絕對如此。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-111">While this can be true, it's not an absolute.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-112">在某些情況下，雲端的運作成本其實較高。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-112">In some cases, cloud operating costs can actually be higher.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-113">這通常導因於成本管控不佳、系統架構不一致、程序重複、系統設定異常，或人事成本增加。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-113">Often this is caused by poor cost governance, misalignment of system architectures, duplication of processes, unusual system configurations, or increased staffing costs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-114">幸而，這類問題多半可以獲得某種程度的解決，而產生早期 ROI。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-114">Luckily, many of these problems can be mitigated to create early ROI.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-115">依照[建置商業論證](#building-the-business-justification)中的指導方針操作，將有助於偵測並避免這類失衡問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-115">Following the guidance in [Building the business justification](#building-the-business-justification) can help detect and avoid these misalignments.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-116">了解此處所破解的其他迷思，也可能會有幫助。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-116">Dispelling the other myths listed here can also help.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7b885-123">**迷思：以內部部署環境作為對照，有助於節省雲端環境的成本。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-123">**Myth: Mirroring my on-premises environment will help me save money in the cloud.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-124">在數位資產規劃期間，客戶在已佈建的環境中偵測到未使用的容量超過 50% 的案例，是確實存在的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-124">During Digital Estate planning, it's not unheard of for customers to detect unused capacity in excess of 50% of the provisioned environment.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-125">如果依照目前的佈建方式在雲端中進行佈建，將難以實現節省成本的目標。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-125">If assets are provisioned in the cloud to match current provisioning, cost savings will be hard to realize.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-126">請考慮減少部署的資產大小以符合使用模式，而非一昧依循佈建模式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-126">Consider reducing the size of the deployed assets to align with usage patterns, not provisioning patterns.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7b885-127">**迷思：伺服器成本是企業選擇進行雲端移轉的誘因。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-127">**Myth: Server costs drive the business cases for cloud migration.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-128">有時候是如此。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-128">Sometimes this is true.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-129">對於某些公司而言，降低與伺服器有關的持續性資本支出，是很重要的。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-129">For some companies, it's important to reduce ongoing capital expenses related to servers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-130">不過，這取決於許多因素。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-130">However, this depends on several factors.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-131">採用五到八年硬體更新週期的公司，不容易看到其雲端移轉很快就產生報酬。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-131">Companies with a five- to eight-year hardware refresh cycle are unlikely to see fast returns on their cloud migration.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-132">公司若採用標準化或強制執行的更新週期，則可以快速達到損益平衡點。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-132">Companies with standardized or enforced refresh cycles can hit a break-even point quickly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-133">無論是哪一種情況，其他費用可能才是選擇移轉的財務誘因。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-133">In either case, other expenses may be the financial triggers that justify the migration.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-134">以下幾個範例說明僅以伺服器或 VM 的角度考量成本時常會忽略的成本：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-134">The following are a few examples of costs that are commonly overlooked when taking a server-only or VM-only view of costs:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="7b885-138">因運作中斷而造成的商業損失可能很快就會超過硬體或軟體的成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-138">Business losses due to outages can quickly exceed hardware or software costs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-139">如果目前的資料中心不穩定、 工作與業務量化的機會成本或實際的商務成本方面的中斷影響。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-139">If the current datacenter is unstable, work with the business to quantify the effect of outages in terms of opportunity costs or actual business costs.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="7b885-140">環境的成本也很可觀。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-140">Environmental costs can also be significant.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-141">就一般的美國家庭而言，購屋是其預算中最大的投資與最高的成本。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-141">For the average American family, their home is the biggest investment and highest cost in their budget.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-142">這也通常適用於資料中心。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-142">The same is often true for datacenters.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-143">不動產、設備和公共設施在其內部部署成本中佔了相當高的比例。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-143">Real estate, facilities, and utility costs represent a fair portion of on-premises costs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-144">當資料中心淘汰時，可以重新規劃這些設施的企業，或可能企業無法釋出成本完全。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-144">When datacenters are retired, those facilities can be repurposed by the business, or potentially the business could be released from the costs entirely.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7b885-145">**迷思：營運費用 (OpEx) 優於資本支出 (CapEx)。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-145">**Myth: Operating Expense (OpEx) is better than Capital Expense (CapEx).**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-146">如[財會結果](business-outcomes/fiscal-outcomes.md)一文所說明，OpEx 可能是良性費用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-146">As explained in the [fiscal outcomes](business-outcomes/fiscal-outcomes.md) article, OpEx can be a good thing.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-147">不過，有許多產業仍將 OpEx 視為減項。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-147">However, there are a number of industries that can see OpEx as a negative.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-148">以下幾個範例所說明的案例，會在 OpEx 方面促使與會計和業務單位緊密整合：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-148">The following are a few examples that would trigger tighter integration with the accounting and business units regarding the OpEx conversation:</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="7b885-151">私人股權公司所擁有或尋求注資的企業，也可能會將 OpEx 增加視為負面結果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-151">OpEx increases can also be seen as a negative outcome in businesses owned by a private equity firm or seeking capital influx.</span></span>
- <span data-ttu-id="7b885-152">如果企業致力於改善銷售毛利或降低銷貨成本 (COGS)，OpEx 也可能是負面結果。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-152">If the business is focused heavily on improving sales margins or reducing Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), the OpEx could be a negative outcome.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7b885-153">OpEx 不一定是壞事。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-153">OpEx is not always a bad thing.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-154">相較於 CapEx，企業將 OpEx 視為有利的可能性會比較高。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-154">Businesses are more likely to see OpEx as more favorable than CapEx.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-155">例如，如果企業嘗試提升現金流量、降低資本投資或減持資產，他們對此舉措的接受度就會很高。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-155">For instance, this approach can be well received by businesses that are attempting to improve cash flow, reduce capital investments, or decrease asset holdings.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7b885-156">在提供聚焦於從 CapEx 轉換到 OpEx 的商業論證之前，請先了解何者對企業較有利。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-156">Before providing a business justification that focuses on a conversion from CapEx to OpEx, understand which is better for the business.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-157">會計和採購通常最能透露出符合財務目標的訊息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-157">Accounting and procurement can often help best align the message to financial objectives.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="7b885-158">**迷思：移轉至雲端就像按開關一樣容易。**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-158">**Myth: Moving to the cloud is like flipping a switch.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-159">移轉是需要大量手動操作的技術性轉換。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-159">Migrations are a manually intense technical transformation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="7b885-160">在擬定商業論證時 (特別是具時效性的論證)，請考量下列可能會拉長資產移轉所需時間的層面：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="7b885-160">When developing a business justification, especially justifications that are time sensitive, consider the following aspects that could increase the time it takes to migrate assets:</span></span>
