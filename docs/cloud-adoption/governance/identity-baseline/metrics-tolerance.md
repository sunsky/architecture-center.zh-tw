## <a name="risk-tolerance-indicators"></a><span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-134">風險承受度指標</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-134">Risk tolerance indicators</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-135">與身分識別基準相關的風險主要與組織的身分識別基礎結構複雜性相關。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-135">Risks related to Identity Baseline are largely related to the complexity of your organization's identity infrastructure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-136">如果您的所有使用者和群組皆使用單一目錄來管理，或雲端原生識別提供者與其他服務進行最低限度的整合，則您的風險層級可能比較小。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-136">If all of your users and groups are managed using a single directory or cloud native identity provider using minimal integration with other services, your risk level will likely be small.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-137">不過，當您的業務需求增加時，您的身分識別基準系統就可能需要支援更複雜的案例，例如需要多個目錄來支援您的內部組織，或與外部識別提供者同盟。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-137">However, as your business needs grow your Identity Baseline systems may need to support more complicated scenarios, such as multiple directories to support your internal organization or federation with external identity providers.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-138">當這些系統越來越複雜時，風險就會增加。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-138">As these systems become more complex, risk increases.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-139">在雲端採用的早期階段，您可以與 IT 安全性小組和業務專案關係人合作，以識別與身分識別相關的[業務風險](business-risks.md)，然後決定可接受的身分識別風險承受度基準。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-139">In the early stages of cloud adoption, work with your IT security team and business stakeholders to identify [business risks](business-risks.md) related to identity, then determine an acceptable baseline for identity risk tolerance.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-140">這一節的 CAF 指引會提供範例，但您公司或部署的詳細風險和基準可能會有所不同。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-140">This section of the CAF guidance provides examples, but the detailed risks and baselines for your company or deployments may be different.</span></span>

- <span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-144">**使用者帳戶數量觸發條件**。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-144">**User account number trigger**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="fd7ca-145">當公司身分識別系統中管理的使用者、群組或其他物件超過某數量時，即可透過身分識別基準專業領域的投資，來確保可以有效率地治理大量帳戶。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="fd7ca-145">A company with more than X number of users, groups, or other objects managed in your identity systems could benefit from investment in the Identity Baseline discipline to ensure efficient governance over a large number of accounts.</span></span>
